My Interest in IT

My interest in IT is mostly to do with aesthetics and functionality. People have made programs which assist creative efforts, but often they end up forcing you to express yourself according to a certain structure. It's paradoxical. Or their interfaces are not as intuitive as they could be. Learning coding would give me the understanding necessary to express myself with multimedia content on a truer/more fundamental level. There are several reasons I chose to come to RMIT - high-calibre course content, its high student intake (and output into the business world) and reputation as a higher education provider. During my studies I expect to continue to learn about the world and acquire a roadmap of the history of IT, its use in the business world, some possible personal opportunities and modern social/ethical issues such as the impact of AI, etc.
